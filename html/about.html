About Us

Power Gym is a health and fitness center that provides the community a family orientated, motivational, friendly and professional environment to improve health and well being.

At Power Gym, we are focused on providing members all the necessary tools to achieve their health and fitness goals.

We believe in training with purpose and intent. We believe in training hard in the right way. We believe in helping people to achieve the strongest version of themselves possible. It is about people, connection, community, fun, and empowerment. It is about helping people to move from where they are now, to a fitter, stronger, healthier future with fewer limitations and greater self-belief.

Why join the power gym?

Training with us is fun and varied

  • ​Become part of a super friendly and social community that has lots of FUN getting fit.​
  • Lose body fat, feel more toned with more energy and vitality.
  • You will become stronger and more conditioned and with it, more confident.
  • Many people come to Train Strong with a specific goal in mind and then go on to achieve far more than they ever dreamed possible.
  • Our range of services caters for everyone and all levels of ability.
  • Receive nutritional guidance for free.
